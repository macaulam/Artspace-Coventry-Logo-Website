Artspace is an approachable and experimental visual arts organisation based in Coventry.

We work with artists and communities to explore and respond to our city’s most unique environments and characteristics and to think about its future as a centre for inspirational artistic engagement.

Our artistic programme exists to create a catalyst for exchange between experienced artists and local audiences and to jointly explore themes relevant to Coventry; creating new visual art work for the city and the visual arts movement in the process.

Alongside our artistic programme, we manage artist development opportunities including studio provision, a membership scheme and volunteering.

We also run outreach projects connecting our programme and projects with people from all walks of life in Coventry and contribute to strategic projects strengthening the local, regional and national visual arts offer.
